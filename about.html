About SSR

SSR Manufacturing Corporation creates manufacturing, engineering, and service efficiency for the semiconductor and other manufacturing industries, helping customers increase productivity and improve system integration and installation service effectively. In addition to best in hardware installation and customer filed services that optimize manufacturing equipment, factory productivity and company efficiency.

1983-1991: Intelmatec Corporation

1991-2002: Automation Component Technology

2002-Present: SSR Manufacturing Corporation

Company History

Founded in 1983, the Intelmatec Corporation began as a system engineering for automations in semiconductor factories and cleanroom building contractor specializing in semiconductor factory interiors for such notable clients as like Intel and Western Digital in Silicon Valley, California.

After Changing company name to SSR MFG. Corporation in 2002, the SSR expanded business scope to General Construction. Our expertise has grown to include quality construction for high-end retail stores, restaurants, offices and hotels. Today, SSR's services also include structural and seismic work, which allows us to offer our clientele a complete range of services from structural upgrades to high quality finishes. We have successfully completed countless projects in San Francisco and the West Coast. As a result, SSR enjoys an excellent reputation for quality coupled with efficiency and timely completion of projects.

SSR Mission Statement

SSR's goal is to be at the forefront of the building industry by our committment to continually developing innovative approaches to construction. We provide a unique work environment that promotes fun, creativity and above all, a passion to excel.
