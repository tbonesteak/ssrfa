services



SSR service and support network spans in USA and Asia. Whenever you need technical support, installation, field service, maintenance, integration support, we will put our local team of highly trained engineers to work to make sure your issues are resolved quickly and efficiently.

SSR's service capabilities include installation, implementation, integration, troubleshooting and correcting issues that cause unscheduled downtime and complex factory system, providing higher tool automation utilization.

SSR also provides hardware and software service in semiconductor, automobile, warehouse, and manufacturing industries.

SSR expands company service capabilities included system installation, implementation, unit test, function test, cycle test, system integration, OEM manufacturing, manufacturing frame assembly/welding/machining, final system communication test and validation, software system support, AMHS capacity analysis and simulation service for factory automation system.
